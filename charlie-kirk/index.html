Charlie Kirk

(1993 – 10 September 2025)

Charlie Kirk was a conservative activist and the founder of Turning Point USA. He became a prominent public speaker and media host, focusing on youth engagement and politics in America. He passed away on 10 September 2025. We remember his influence and pray for his family, friends, and all impacted by his life and work.
