FAQ & Helpful Links

What is the Gospel?

The good news that Jesus Christ lived, died, and rose again so that we could be forgiven and have eternal life.

How do I become a Christian?

By turning from sin and trusting Jesus as Lord and Savior (Romans 10:9).

Where to start reading the Bible

We recommend the Gospel of John for newcomers who want to learn about Jesus.
